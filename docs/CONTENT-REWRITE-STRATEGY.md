## Blog Content Strategy

### Categories to Reframe

| ❌ OLD CATEGORY | ✅ NEW CATEGORY |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Jailbroken Fire Stick Guides | Streaming Device Setup Guides |
| IPTV Tutorials | Live TV Streaming Tips |
| Free Streaming | Smart Streaming Solutions |
| Cord Cutting | Streaming Independence |

### Blog Title Rewrites (Examples)

| ❌ OLD TITLE | ✅ NEW TITLE |
|--------------|---------------|
| "What is a Jailbroken Fire Stick?" | "What is a Pre-Configured Streaming Device?" |
| "Best IPTV Services 2025" | "Best Live TV Streaming Options 2025" |
| "How to Jailbreak Fire Stick" | "How to Set Up Your Streaming Device" |
| "Free PPV on Fire Stick" | "Watch Live Sports Events on Fire Stick" |
| "IPTV vs Cable Cost Comparison" | "Streaming vs Cable: Complete Cost Guide" |
| "Watch NFL Free on Fire Stick" | "Best Ways to Stream NFL Games" |
| "Best Free Streaming Apps" | "Top Streaming Apps for Fire Stick" |

### Content Guidelines

1. **Remove all "free" claims** - Use "included" or "access" instead
2. **Remove channel counts** - Use "extensive library" or "comprehensive coverage"
3. **Remove PPV references** - Use "live events" or "premium sports"
4. **Add disclaimers** - "Content availability varies by region and service"
5. **Emphasize support** - Highlight customer service, setup help, troubleshooting
6. **Add expertise signals** - Author bios, "reviewed by" tags, update dates
