COPY-PASTE FILENAMES FOR SUPABASE STORAGE UPLOAD
==================================================

When uploading images to your "public" bucket in Supabase Storage, use these EXACT filenames:

1. HERO BACKGROUND IMAGE:
   hero-firestick-breakout.jpg

2. FIRE STICK PRODUCT IMAGES (upload in this order):
   firestick-hd.jpg
   firestick-4k.jpg
   firestick-4k-max.jpg

3. IPTV SUBSCRIPTION IMAGE (if you have one):
   iptv-subscription.jpg

---
INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Click "Upload file" in your "public" bucket
2. Select your image file
3. In the filename field, DELETE the original filename
4. PASTE one of the filenames above (exactly as shown)
5. Click "Upload"
6. Repeat for each image
