## SEO Patterns from Top Competitors

### Content Strategies
- ✅ Fresh dates in titles ("December 2025", "2025 updated")
- ✅ Long-form guides (2,000+ words with screenshots/video)
- ✅ Step-by-step numbered format with visuals
- ✅ Device compatibility mentions (Fire TV Stick 4K Max, Lite, Cube)
- ✅ Legal disclaimers for liability protection
- ✅ VPN recommendations integrated throughout

### Technical SEO
- Fast loading times
- Mobile-responsive design
- Schema markup (HowTo, FAQ)
- Strong internal linking
- Regular content updates

### Monetization
- VPN affiliate programs (NordVPN, Surfshark, ExpressVPN)
- Product sales
- Patreon/memberships
- YouTube channel integration
