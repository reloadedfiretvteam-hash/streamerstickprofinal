## On-Page SEO Requirements for Each Post

### Title Tags
- Format: `{Primary Keyword} | StreamStickPro`
- Keep under 60 characters
- Include year for freshness

### Meta Descriptions
- 150-160 characters
- Include primary keyword naturally
- Include CTA phrase ("Learn how", "Discover", "Get started")

### Header Structure
- H1: Post title (exact or close match to target keyword)
- H2: Major sections (include secondary keywords)
- H3: Subsections
- Use keyword variations in headers

### Content Requirements
- Minimum 1,500 words (2,000+ preferred)
- Include numbered lists and bullet points
- Add internal links to products and other blog posts
- Include at least 1 FAQ section (for schema)
- Add device compatibility mentions
- Include VPN recommendation section (potential affiliate revenue)

### Schema Markup
- HowTo schema for tutorial posts
- FAQ schema for all posts (3-5 questions)
- Article schema with author and date
- Product schema when discussing specific products

### Internal Linking
- Link to 2-3 related blog posts
- Link to 1-2 product pages
- Link to main store from each post
- Use descriptive anchor text with keywords

## Content Differentiation Strategy

### What Makes Our Content Unique:
1. **Bundle Focus** - Only site offering Fire Stick + IPTV combinations
2. **Sports Specialization** - Deep coverage of UFC, NFL, NBA, MLB
3. **Premium Quality Angle** - "Skip the DIY hassle" messaging
4. **Setup Support** - Reference our included setup videos
5. **US Market Focus** - Specifically targeting American cord-cutters

### Avoid:
- Generic "best apps for Fire Stick" content (TroyPoint owns this)
- Pure technical jailbreak tutorials (legal risk)
- APK download links (policy violations)
- Unsubstantiated claims about stream quality
