## Content Structure Template

Each piece follows this structure:

1. **Title** (H1): Question or comparison format with year
2. **Meta Description**: 155-160 characters, keyword-rich, compelling
3. **Introduction**: Problem statement, hook, value proposition
4. **Overview Section**: What readers will learn
5. **Comparison Tables**: Visual comparisons where applicable
6. **Detailed Sections**: 
   - Features breakdown
   - Pros and cons
   - Use case recommendations
   - Setup instructions (where applicable)
7. **Recommendations Section**: Natural product/service mentions
8. **FAQ Section**: 5-10 common questions with answers
9. **Conclusion**: Summary and call-to-action
10. **Internal Links**: 3-5 natural links to StreamStickPro pages
11. **Related Content**: Links to related guides/posts

## SEO Optimization Per Piece

- **Target Keyword**: Primary keyword in title, first paragraph, H2 headings
- **Secondary Keywords**: Natural usage throughout content
- **Word Count**: 1,500-2,500 words (comprehensive, valuable content)
- **Images**: 3-5 relevant images with optimized alt text
- **Internal Links**: 3-5 links to StreamStickPro pages
- **External Links**: 2-3 links to authoritative sources
- **Schema Markup**: HowTo, Comparison, FAQ schema where applicable
- **Meta Tags**: Optimized title, description, keywords
- **URL Structure**: /guides/[keyword-friendly-slug]
