## STEP 1: Go to the Template Project

## STEP 2: Go to Domains Tab

1. **Click the "Domains" tab** in the template project
2. **You should see your domain listed there**

3. **Tell me:** "Step 2 done" or "I see my domain"

---

## STEP 3: Remove Domain from Template Project

1. **Find your domain** in the list
2. **Look for a button** next to it that says:
   - "Remove" or
   - "Disconnect" or
   - "Delete" or
   - Three dots (...) (click those)

3. **Click that button**

4. **Confirm** if it asks "Are you sure?"

5. **Tell me:** "Step 3 done" or "I removed the domain"

---

## STEP 4: Go Back to Your Real Project

1. **Go back to the Cloudflare Pages dashboard**
2. **Click on the project** with your real code (the one we just set up)
3. **Go to "Domains" tab**

4. **Tell me:** "Step 4 done" or "I'm on the real project"

---

## STEP 5: Add Domain to Real Project

1. **Click "Add custom domain"** (or similar button)
2. **Enter your domain name**
3. **Click "Add" or "Continue"**

4. **Tell me:** "Step 5 done" or "I added the domain"
