## Brand Guidelines:
- **Primary Colors**: Blue (#0066FF), Purple (#6633FF)
- **Secondary Colors**: Orange (#FF6600), Green (#00CC66)
- **Font**: Bold, modern sans-serif (Montserrat, Poppins, or similar)
- **Logo**: StreamStickPro.com should be clearly visible
- **Style**: Modern, tech-forward, professional

## Image Creation Tools:
1. **AI Generators**: DALL-E, Midjourney, Stable Diffusion, Leonardo.ai
2. **Design Tools**: Canva, Adobe Express, Figma
3. **Stock Photos**: Unsplash, Pexels (for base images)
4. **Animation**: After Effects, Canva Animations, Lottie
