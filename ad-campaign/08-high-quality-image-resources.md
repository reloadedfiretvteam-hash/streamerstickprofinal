## üìã IMAGE SPECIFICATIONS:

### For TikTok/YouTube Shorts:
- **Size**: 1080x1920 pixels (vertical)
- **Format**: PNG (with transparency) or JPG
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI minimum
- **File Size**: Under 5MB per image
- **Color Space**: RGB
- **Background**: Transparent or solid color

### For Google Ads:
- **Size**: 1080x1080 (square) or 1080x1920 (vertical)
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Resolution**: 300 DPI
- **File Size**: Under 5MB
