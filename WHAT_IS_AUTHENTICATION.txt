Authentication = Login System

It's like having a lock on your front door:
- Without the key (username/password) = Can't get in
- With the key (username/password) = Can get in

┌───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ HOW IT WORKS FOR YOUR ADMIN PANEL:                    │
└───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

1. You click "Admin Login" button
2. You enter your username and password
3. The system checks if they match
4. If they match → You get access to admin panel
5. If they don't match → You get an error
